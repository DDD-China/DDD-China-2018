Notice

Receipt registration and process

Only paper based receipts are supported

  1. After the confirmation of the payment received;
  2. Please make sure you register with the right information for your receipts. For receipt correction, please email to tickets@ddd-china.com;
  3. Under normal circumstances, receipts will be issued and mailed within 2 weeks upon successful payment;
  4. By the conference committee, the full amount of the successful payment is subject to the receipt. If you have any special request, please contact tickets@ddd-china.com as soon as possible.
  5. All receipts of this conference will be issued through the authorized 3rd party. The receipt can be issued under both legal modes for “technical development”, “technical consulting”, “management consulting”, or “conference participation”. Please choose the subject according to your need.

Special notice

Please read the following notices carefully:

  1. The tickets are NONE refundable.
  2. If you cannot attend the conference, and transferred the ticket to other people, please notify us 10 days before the actual conference through email tickets@ddd-china.com to change the participates. We won’t be able to process any request afterwards.
  3. E-ticket is the only proof to register on site. Please make sure you show your ticket to be admitted into the conference.
    4. Under unforeseeable circumstances, if the conference was cancelled, the conference committee will refund all your payment.
