Cloud Time | DDD China Conference 2018

The 2nd Domain Driven Design China Summit will be held again at the Beijing International Convention Center from November 29th to December 1st, 2018. We will invite leaders from DDD global communities in Europe and North America, as well as representatives from many domestic teams who have dedicated their efforts to implement DDD in the past years to join us, and share DDD practices, latest developments in theory, and practical experiences.
